**1. Introduction** 🚀
Notioff ("the App", "we", "us") is an Android application that helps users filter and manage their device notifications. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is used.

---

**2. Permissions We Request** 🔑

**Notification Access** (`BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE`)
* The App requires permission to read your notifications in order to apply your filtering rules.
* This is the core function of the App.
* You can revoke this permission at any time in Android Settings → Notification access.

---

**3. Data Collection and Processing** 📊

**3.1 Notification Content** 💬
* Your notification content (app name, title, body text) is read locally on your device to match against your filtering rules.
* **Notification content is never stored permanently.**
* Notification content is **never sent to our servers.**

**3.2 AI-Powered Filtering (Optional Feature)** 🤖
* If you enable AI-based filtering, the title and body text of incoming notifications are sent to **Anthropic's Claude API** for classification.
* This transmission happens over an encrypted HTTPS connection.
* Anthropic may process and temporarily store this data in accordance with their own privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
* **We do not store AI request/response data on our servers.**
* You can disable AI filtering at any time in the App settings. When disabled, no notification content leaves your device.
* We strongly recommend reviewing which apps' notifications are subject to AI filtering and excluding sensitive apps (e.g. banking, messaging) if you are concerned.

**3.3 User-Created Filtering Rules** ⚙️
* Rules you create (keywords, app selections, conditions) are stored **locally on your device** using an encrypted local database (Room).
* These rules are never uploaded to any server.

**3.4 Subscription and Purchase Data** 💳
* Subscription management is handled by **RevenueCat, Inc.**
* When you make a purchase, RevenueCat processes your Google Play receipt to verify subscription status.
* We receive only an anonymized subscriber ID and subscription status — never your payment details.
* RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

**3.5 Crash Reports and Analytics** 🐛
* The App may collect anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs.
* These reports do not contain notification content, personal messages, or identifying information.

---

**4. Data We Do NOT Collect** 🚫
* We do **not** collect your name, email address, or any account information.
* We do **not** store notification content on any server.
* We do **not** sell any data to third parties.
* We do **not** use your data for advertising purposes.
* We do **not** share your data with any party other than those listed in Section 3.

---

**5. Data Storage and Security** 🔒
* All user preferences and filtering rules are stored locally on your device using Android's Room database.
* Data transmitted to Anthropic or RevenueCat is protected via HTTPS/TLS.
* We do not operate any backend infrastructure that stores user data.

---

**6. Children's Privacy** 🧸
The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us.

---

**7. Your Rights** ⚖️
Depending on your country of residence, you may have the right to:
* Access the data we hold about you
* Request deletion of your data
* Withdraw consent for AI processing at any time (via App settings)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: [boranjobs@icloud.com]

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it.
